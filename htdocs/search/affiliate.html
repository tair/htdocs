Making Affiliations

Affiliating yourself to a lab or organization

You can affiliate yourself yourself to a lab or other organization in TAIR's community. If you are the head of a lab (PI) or contact person for a lab or organization, you can also add and remove affiliated people from your organization/lab.

Search for your lab or organization in TAIR

To affiliate yourself to a lab or organization, the lab/organization must exist in TAIR's community. To find a lab from your personal profile information page, type in the LAST name of your PI ( or instructor or group leader) in the input box and click on 'Search and add me to a lab'. To find an organization in TAIR's database, type in the name of the organization in the section 'Other Organization Affiliations' and click on 'Search and Add Me to an Organization'.This search is a wild card search (e.g. it will search for labs/organizations containing the search term).

What to do if your lab/organization is not found.

If the the lab you wish to affiliate yourself is not found, with check the spelling to make sure your parameters are correct. If the record does not exist in TAIR, please ask your PI to add her/his lab to TAIR's community. If you are trying to find an organization and it is not in TAIR, you may click on 'Add New Organization'. See Adding Organizations for more information.

Affiliating yourself to a lab

If your search retrieves a record for the lab, you can affiliate yourself by checking the box under the header 'Affiliate' in the row corresponding to the correct lab. You can also define your relationship to the lab (e.g. job title) by selecting a job title from the drop down menu. After you check the appropriate box click on the 'Make Affiliations' button.

Affiliating yourself to an organization

If your search retrieves a record for the organization, you can affiliate yourself by checking the box under the header 'Affiliate' in the row corresponding to the correct organization. You can also define your relationship to the organization (e.g. job title) by selecting a job title from the drop down menu. If you are the contact person for the organization, click on the check box After you check the appropriate box click on the 'Make Affiliations' button.

Affiliating People/Organizations to your lab or organization

If you are the PI of a lab or contact person for any organization, you can affiliate people to your organization/lab.

Finding people to affiliate to your lab or organization

You can affiliate people who are registered with TAIR to your lab/organization. From the lab/organization profile page in the section titled, Affiliated Persons, FOR EACH person you would like to affiliate, type in the person's LAST name and click on the Search and Add Members button.

If the person is registered at TAIR, click on the 'Affiliate' check box next to the person's name/country and then click on the 'Make Affiliation' button to add that person to your lab/organization. You can also designate a person as a contact person (they will be able to update the organization and affiliations) and select a job title for that person.

Affiliating other organizations to your organization

If are the head of a lab or a contact person you can affiliate other organizations in TAIR's community to your lab/organization. From the lab/organization profile page in the section titled, Affiliated Organizations, FOR EACH organization you would like to affiliate, type in the name of the organization and click on the Search and Add Affiliate Organization button.

If the organization in TAIR's database click on the 'Affiliate' check box for the appropriate organization and then click on the 'Make Affiliations' button to affiliate the organization to your lab/organization.
